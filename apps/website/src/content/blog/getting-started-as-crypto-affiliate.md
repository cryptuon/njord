**TL;DR:** To start earning as a crypto affiliate on Njord Protocol: (1) connect a Solana wallet, (2) create your on-chain affiliate profile, (3) browse and join campaigns, (4) generate unique tracking links, (5) promote and earn instant commissions. No application process, no minimum traffic — just connect and start.

## What is a Crypto Affiliate?

A crypto affiliate is a promoter who earns cryptocurrency commissions for driving sales, leads, or actions for companies using a blockchain-based affiliate protocol. Unlike traditional affiliate marketing, crypto affiliates receive payments instantly to their wallet rather than waiting 30-90 days for bank transfers.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you'll need:

1. **A Solana wallet**: Phantom, Solflare, or any Solana-compatible wallet
2. **A small amount of SOL**: For transaction fees (less than $0.01 per transaction)
3. **An audience**: A blog, social media following, YouTube channel, newsletter, or community

That's it. No application, no approval process, no minimum traffic requirements.

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Visit the [Njord Protocol Dashboard](/dashboard) and connect your Solana wallet. This establishes your identity on the protocol — your wallet address is your unique identifier.

## Step 2: Create Your Affiliate Profile

Once connected, create your on-chain affiliate profile. This records your participation in the protocol and establishes your starting tier.

### Understanding Affiliate Tiers

Your tier determines which campaigns you can access and how quickly you receive payouts:

| Tier | NJORD Stake | Hold Period | Campaign Access |
|------|------------|-------------|-----------------|
| New | 0 NJORD | 7 days | Open campaigns only |
| Verified | 100 NJORD | 3 days | Standard campaigns |
| Trusted | 1,000 NJORD | 1 day | Premium campaigns |
| Elite | 10,000 NJORD | 0 days | All campaigns, instant settlement |

You start at the "New" tier with no staking requirement. As you earn and stake NJORD tokens, you unlock higher tiers with better campaigns and faster payouts.

## Step 3: Browse Campaigns

The [Campaigns page](/campaigns) shows all active campaigns on the protocol. Each listing includes:

- **Commission rate**: The percentage or fixed amount you earn per conversion
- **Campaign budget**: How much funding remains in the escrow
- **Minimum tier**: The affiliate tier required to participate
- **Conversion type**: What counts as a conversion (sale, lead, signup, etc.)

Filter campaigns by category, commission rate, or minimum tier to find the best fit for your audience.

## Step 4: Join a Campaign and Generate Links

When you find a campaign that matches your audience, join it to generate your unique tracking link. This link contains your affiliate PDA (Program Derived Address) — a cryptographic identifier that ties conversions back to your wallet.

Every time someone clicks your link and completes a conversion, the event is recorded on-chain and attributed to you.

## Step 5: Promote and Earn

Share your tracking links through your preferred channels:

- **Blog posts**: Write reviews, comparisons, or tutorials featuring the product
- **Social media**: Share with your audience on Twitter, YouTube, TikTok, etc.
- **Email newsletters**: Include affiliate links in relevant content
- **Communities**: Share in Discord servers, Telegram groups, or forums (where permitted)

### Commission Payouts

When a conversion is verified, your commission is automatically transferred from the campaign's escrow account to your wallet. Depending on your tier, there may be a short hold period before the funds are released.

At the Elite tier, settlement is instant — your commission arrives in ~3 seconds.

## Tips for Success

1. **Promote products you've used**: Authentic recommendations convert better than generic promotions
2. **Match campaigns to your audience**: A DeFi audience wants DeFi campaigns, not random products
3. **Stake NJORD to upgrade tiers**: Higher tiers unlock better campaigns and faster payouts
4. **Track your performance**: Monitor your conversion rates and optimize your promotion strategy
5. **Build your on-chain reputation**: Consistent, fraud-free performance builds trust in the protocol

## How Much Can You Earn?

Earnings depend on your audience size, conversion rates, and the campaigns you promote. Commission rates on Njord Protocol are set by individual campaigns and typically range from 5% to 30% of conversion value.

Because Njord Protocol only charges a 2.5% protocol fee (compared to 15-30% at traditional networks), more of the commission budget goes directly to affiliates.
