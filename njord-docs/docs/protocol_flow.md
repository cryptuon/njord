# Understanding the Protocol Flow
This section provides a detailed overview of the Affiliate and Brand Marketing Web3 Protocol flow, from the initialization of a campaign by a company to the final payout of commissions.

## Overview of the Flow
The protocol follows a straightforward flow involving four main parties:

Company Initiation: The company initialises a smart contract with campaign parameters. This includes details like budget, targets, and other specifics of the campaign.

Affiliate Signup: Affiliates can browse the campaigns that are public on the protocol's website and signup to participate in a campaign that aligns with their interests and capabilities.

Customer Interaction: End customers use the affiliate-specific link or data to purchase or view the company's product or service. Their interaction with the smart contract is tracked for on-chain attribution to the affiliate.

Commission Payout: At the end of the campaign or based on the conditions set in the smart contract, the commission is paid out to the affiliate as per the initial setup by the company.

Budget Extension: If desired, the company can extend the campaign by increasing the budget and sending more funds to the smart contract.
