# Stakeholders
## Protocol Creators
The protocol creators are the architects and developers of the Affiliate and Brand Marketing Web3 Protocol. They are responsible for designing, developing, deploying, and maintaining the protocol. They also handle the listing of campaigns for affiliates and receive payment from campaign smart contracts for this service.

## Companies
Companies are business entities that wish to leverage the protocol for their affiliate marketing campaigns. They initialize smart contracts, defining their campaign parameters such as budget, targets, and other details. Companies can adjust these parameters and extend the budget as required during the course of the campaign.

## Affiliates
Affiliates are individuals or entities who partner with companies to promote their products or services. They browse the listed campaigns, sign up for participation, and use affiliate-specific data for promotion. Affiliates earn a commission from the sales or traffic they generate, as per the terms set in the smart contract by the companies.

## End Customers
End customers are the consumers who purchase or view the company's products or services through the affiliate-specific links or data. They interact with the smart contract by using their wallets, enabling on-chain attribution of their actions to the specific affiliate. End customers form the core of the affiliate marketing cycle as their actions determine the success of the campaigns and the commissions for affiliates.
